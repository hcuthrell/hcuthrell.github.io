The App 

Downloadable from the App Store for $1.99, SPLITZ instantly connects consumers to a variety of business and brands. With one tap, a login username and password, and credit/debit card(s) information, those who enjoy the convenience of online shopping can now enjoy the convenience of easy paying, as well.
